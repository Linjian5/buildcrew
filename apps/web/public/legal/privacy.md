# BuildCrew Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 24, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

BuildCrew ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use BuildCrew ("Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

| Data | Purpose | Required |
|------|---------|----------|
| Email address | Account authentication | Yes |
| Name | Display in the app | Yes |
| Password | Account security (stored as bcrypt hash) | Yes |
| Payment information | Subscription billing (processed by Stripe, not stored by us) | For paid plans |

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

With your consent (you can opt out in Settings):

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Feature usage frequency | Product improvement |
| Number of agents/companies (counts only) | Capacity planning |
| AI model provider distribution | Industry insights |
| App version and OS | Compatibility |
| Crash reports (sanitized stack traces) | Bug fixing |

### 2.3 Information We NEVER Collect

We want to be absolutely clear about what we do NOT collect:

- ❌ Your code or AI-generated outputs
- ❌ Your AI model API keys
- ❌ Your conversation content with AI agents
- ❌ Your task descriptions or details
- ❌ Your Knowledge Hub entries
- ❌ Your company names, agent names, or mission statements
- ❌ Your files or documents
- ❌ Any content that could identify your business or projects

## 3. How Your Data Is Stored

### 3.1 Local Data (Your Machine)

The following data is stored exclusively on your local machine and never transmitted to our servers:

- Company configurations and data
- Agent configurations, status, and performance
- All tasks, conversations, and chat history
- Knowledge Hub entries
- Budget and usage details
- Guardian alerts and policies
- Review Pipeline data
- Your AI model API keys (AES-256-GCM encrypted)
- All AI-generated code and content

### 3.2 Cloud Data (Our Servers)

The following data is stored on our servers:

- Account information (email, name, password hash)
- Subscription status and billing records
- Anonymous usage statistics (if you opt in)
- Device activation records (for license management)

Our servers are hosted on reputable cloud providers with industry-standard security measures.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Account authentication | Contract necessity |
| Subscription management | Contract necessity |
| License validation | Contract necessity |
| Product improvement (anonymous) | Your consent (opt-out available) |
| Bug fixing (crash reports) | Your consent (opt-out available) |
| Security and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest |
| Communication (updates, billing) | Contract necessity |

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We share data only in these circumstances:

- **Payment processor** (Stripe): To process subscription payments. Stripe's privacy policy applies.
- **Legal requirements**: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Business transfer**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with advance notice to you.

We do NOT share your data with:
- Advertising networks
- Data brokers
- AI model providers (your API keys go directly from your machine to the provider)

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
- **Correction**: Update your account information at any time in Settings.
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated cloud data in Settings → Profile → Delete Account.
- **Data Export**: Export your local data at any time (it's already on your machine).
- **Opt Out**: Disable anonymous telemetry in Settings → Privacy.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke consent for optional data collection at any time.

### For EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

In addition to the above, you have the right to:
- Data portability
- Restriction of processing
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

### For California Users (CCPA)

You have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## 7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- API keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your local machine
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt
- All cloud communications use HTTPS/TLS 1.3
- Cloud data is encrypted at rest
- Access controls and audit logging on our servers
- Regular security assessments

## 8. Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
- **Subscription records**: Retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance.
- **Anonymous telemetry**: Aggregated data retained indefinitely. Individual records deleted after 90 days.
- **Local data**: Remains on your machine. We have no control over it.

## 9. Children's Privacy

BuildCrew is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

## 10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your account data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We use standard contractual clauses and appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

## 11. Cookies

BuildCrew uses only essential cookies/local storage:

| Cookie/Storage | Purpose | Duration |
|----------------|---------|----------|
| Authentication token | Keep you logged in | Session / 7 days |
| Language preference | Remember your language choice | Persistent |
| Theme preference | Remember dark/light mode | Persistent |

We do NOT use:
- Third-party tracking cookies
- Advertising cookies
- Analytics cookies (we use privacy-friendly server-side analytics)

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
